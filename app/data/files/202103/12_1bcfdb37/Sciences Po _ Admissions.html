In what context do you know the candidate? Do not exceed 2000 characters.

-I know Christopher Liberty from the work we implemented through an organisation he worked for in Kabale district . the organisation is known as Kabale Women in Development . This district has very hard to reach areas but Liberty had the passion to take the health advocacy agenda to those that most need affordable accessible and timely health services. He also Won the Young Researchers’ Scholarship Award 2019 to present at the Africa Health Agenda International Conference. He is a go getter and young person who is very knowledgable about data. he was the lead M and E officer in this projects covering the district of Kabale.
